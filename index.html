What We Offer

As a tivated member you will enjoy the following:

Tasks

Organize your life by creating a todo list. You can create as many tasks as your want and even schedule them in the future.

Earn tivated points

Each task completed earns tivated points for that paricular task.

Redeem Points

You will be able to redeem your hard earned tivated points for anything you want. You can redeem the points for a movie night!

Self Inspiration

Tivated instills self discipline and motivation. You are your own leader. You set the tasks, you complete them and even reward yourself.

Join Tivated Community

Invite friends and family to the tivated community. You can use tivated with friends or family who approve your tasks. They can also be rewarding you after you redeem your tivated points!
